Why Coding?

I graduated with a degree in computer science, but I never pursued a career related to my degree. I struggled through college pursuing my computer science degree. So when I graduated, I struggled through the interviews which lead me to change my career field. Years later, I realized that I struggled through college because I had too many distractions around me, so it was hard for me to stay focused. Now that I am older and more mature, I feel like I am ready to properly learn computer science and try pursue a career in computer engineering. I was first introduced to coding temple by a friend who is currently enrolled in the December 19th session. He told me about his research in coding temple and how they are different from other coding bootcamps. After speaking with my friends and doing some research on my own, I thought Coding Temple would be the perfect fit for me. I never built a solid foundation while studying computer science in college. Thus, my goal in Coding Temple is to build a solid foundation that will help me better understand the world of coding.
